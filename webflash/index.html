1. Connect your P1IB to your computer by USB.

2. Make sure the device is found by your operating system.
Drivers might be needed to be installed for windows users ( http://www.wch-ic.com/downloads/CH341SER_EXE.html )

3. Press the "connect" button below, select correct serial port for your p1ib device and follow the instructions.

4. Wait until finished. Done! The device is now reset to factory settings and have entered WiFi Access Point-mode (AP-mode).
